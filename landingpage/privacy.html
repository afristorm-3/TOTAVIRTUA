Privacy Policy

Thank you for using our Healthcare Accessibility Platform. We are dedicated software developers committed to making healthcare accessible to all through our fun and free platform. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy is designed to help you understand how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our platform.

By using our platform, you agree to the terms and practices described in this Privacy Policy. Please take a moment to read and understand our privacy practices.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We want to emphasize that we do not require any personal information from you, such as your email address, name, or contact details. We do not ask for or store any personally identifiable information.

2. No User Sign-Up or Login Required

Our platform is designed for your convenience. You are not required to create an account, sign up, or log in to use our services. We value your anonymity and ensure that you can access our platform without any unnecessary data collection.

3. Conversations Are Not Recorded

Your conversations with our AI healthcare assistant are private and secure. We do not record, store, or access any conversations between users and the AI. Your healthcare discussions remain confidential, and we do not have any means to retrieve or access them.

4. Cookies and Usage Data

We may collect usage data and employ cookies to improve the performance and functionality of our platform. These cookies are used to enhance your user experience and are not used to track your personal information.

5. Third-Party Links

Our platform may contain links to third-party websites or services that are not owned or controlled by us. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of these third-party sites. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of any third-party websites you visit.

6. Children's Privacy

Our platform is intended for a general audience and is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe that we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us, and we will take steps to delete the information as soon as possible.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page, and the "Last Updated" date at the top of the policy will be revised. We recommend checking this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about our data practices.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or feedback regarding this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at thegenius.den@gmail.com.

Your privacy is important to us, and we are dedicated to providing a safe and secure healthcare accessibility platform for all users.
